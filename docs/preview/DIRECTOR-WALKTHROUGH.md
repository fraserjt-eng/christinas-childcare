# A first look at the simpler daily tools

Hi, here is an early look at a simpler version of the daily tools, the kind of
screens parents and staff would actually use day to day. It is clickable, so
you can tap around and get a feel for it.

A few things to know before you start:
- Everything here is pretend. The families and staff are made up, the photos
  are demo photos, and nothing you tap touches the real site or the real
  records. You cannot break anything.
- It opens on a phone or a computer, whichever is easier.

## A ten minute walk

The first screen is a list of every screen and a step by step walk. If you
just want the highlights, try these:

1. The front door and code pad (the iPad in the lobby). Tap Families, enter
   1234, and check a child in. Add a drop off photo if you like.
2. A parent's phone. Tap "Parent's phone," sign in as a parent, and see their
   kids: who is here, how their day is going, a message from the room, what
   they owe, and the child's allergies and who can pick up.
3. The office. Enter 9999. This is your view. Tap "Edit my buttons" and add
   Billing or Messages. In Billing you can mark a family paid, and it clears
   on that family's phone. In Messages you can write a family and they see it.
4. A teacher's room. Enter a staff code, clock in, add your own photo, then
   open your room to log a meal or a nap.
