### Kiosk Check-In / Check-Out

Every classroom has a tablet kiosk near the entrance. You'll use a 4-digit PIN to check your child in and out each day.

**Your PIN:** ______ (assigned during enrollment)

**How to check in:**
1. Tap the kiosk screen to wake it up
2. Enter your 4-digit PIN
3. Select the child (or children) you're checking in
4. Tap "Check In"
5. You'll see a confirmation with the time recorded

**How to check out:**
1. Enter your 4-digit PIN
2. Select the child you're picking up
3. Tap "Check Out"
4. Confirmation appears with departure time

**Important:**
- Check in when you arrive, check out when you leave. Every time.
- Only authorized adults can check out your child. Update your authorized pickup list in your profile.
- If you forget your PIN, ask the front desk. They can look it up or reset it.

### Updating Your Family Profile

Keeping your profile current protects your child. Here's what to review:

**Go to:** Dashboard > Family Profile

Update these fields:
1. **Allergies and dietary needs** -- List every allergy, even mild ones. Staff rely on this daily.
2. **Emergency contacts** -- At least 2 contacts besides yourself. Include phone numbers and relationship.
3. **Home address** -- Keep current for emergency records.
4. **Authorized pickup list** -- Only people on this list can check out your child. Add name, phone, and relationship.
5. **Medical information** -- Pediatrician name, insurance info, any medications.

**How often:** Review your profile at least once per quarter, and update immediately when anything changes (new phone number, new emergency contact, new allergy diagnosis).

### Messaging Your Child's Teacher

**Go to:** Dashboard > Messages

**To send a message:**
1. Tap "New Message"
2. Select your child's teacher (or "Front Desk" for general questions)
3. Type your message
4. Tap Send

**Response times:** Staff check messages at the start and end of each day. Expect a reply within 4 business hours. For urgent matters (sick child, late pickup), call the center directly.

**Conversation history:** All messages are saved. You can scroll back to review past conversations anytime.

### Notification Settings

**Go to:** Dashboard > Settings > Notifications

Choose which notifications you receive and how:

| Notification Type | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Daily Report Ready | Your child's daily summary is available to view |
| New Photos | Teacher uploaded new activity photos |
| New Message | A staff member sent you a message |
| Schedule Change | Center hours or closures changed |
| Incident Report | Your child was involved in an incident (always on) |

**Tip:** Keep "Incident Report" and "Schedule Change" turned on. These are the ones you don't want to miss.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can both parents have accounts?**
A: Yes. Each parent or guardian can have their own login and their own kiosk PIN. Both will see the same child information.

**Q: What if someone else is picking up my child?**
A: Add them to your Authorized Pickup list in your family profile. They'll need to show ID at the center. If it's a one-time situation, call the front desk in advance.

**Q: How do I see what my child ate today?**
A: Go to Dashboard > Daily Reports. Meal information is updated after each meal service (breakfast, lunch, and snacks).

**Q: Can I message the center on weekends?**
A: You can send messages anytime. Staff will respond during the next business day. For emergencies, use the center phone number.

**Q: How do I change my PIN?**
A: Go to Dashboard > Settings > Kiosk PIN. Enter your current PIN, then set a new 4-digit PIN. Your new PIN works immediately.
