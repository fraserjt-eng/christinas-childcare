### Meal Count Guide

Meal counts are how the center gets reimbursed through CACFP (Child and Adult Care Food Program). Every missed count costs real money. This is one of the most important things you do each day.

**Which meals to count:**
- Breakfast
- AM Snack
- Lunch
- PM Snack

**Deadline Chart:**

| Meal | Serve Window | Submit Deadline | Status if Late |
|---|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | 7:30 - 9:00 AM | 9:30 AM | Yellow warning |
| AM Snack | 9:30 - 10:30 AM | 11:00 AM | Yellow warning |
| Lunch | 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM | 1:30 PM | Yellow warning |
| PM Snack | 2:30 - 3:30 PM | 4:00 PM | Yellow warning |

**How to submit:**
1. Go to your dashboard > Meal Counts
2. Select the meal type and your classroom
3. The system pre-fills children who are checked in. Adjust if any child refused a meal or was absent.
4. Tap Submit
5. Green checkmark = submitted. Red = overdue. Yellow = approaching deadline.

**Rules:**
- Submit within 15 minutes of meal end. Do not wait until end of day.
- If you miss a deadline, submit anyway. Late is better than missing.
- Never estimate. Count actual children who were served.

### Photo Upload Guide

Parents who see daily photos are significantly more likely to stay enrolled. Three to five photos per shift keeps families connected.

**Go to:** Dashboard > Photos > Upload

**Steps:**
1. Tap "Upload Photos"
2. Select up to 5 photos at once (batch upload)
3. For each photo, select an **activity tag**:
   - Free Play
   - Art/Creative
   - Circle Time
   - Outdoor Play
   - Meals/Snack
   - Nap/Rest
   - Learning Activity
   - Special Event
4. Add a brief caption (1 sentence: what's happening, who's involved)
5. Tap Submit

**Tips:**
- Take photos during every activity block. Upload during transitions or nap time.
- Focus on children engaged in activities, not posed shots.
- Check that no restricted children appear in photos (check your classroom's photo permission list).
- Photos go through an approval step before parents see them. Your director reviews and publishes.

### Task Completion Guide

Tasks are organized by time block so you know what to focus on and when.

**Go to:** Dashboard > Tasks

**Time blocks:**
- Morning (7:00 - 11:00 AM)
- Midday (11:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
- Afternoon (1:00 - 3:00 PM)
- Nap Time (specific window based on your classroom)
- End of Day (3:00 - 6:00 PM)

**Priority levels:**
- **High** (red) -- Must complete today. Safety, compliance, or parent-facing.
- **Medium** (yellow) -- Should complete today. Operational tasks.
- **Low** (blue) -- Complete when time allows. Prep and organization tasks.

**How to complete a task:**
1. Tap the task to open details
2. Read the instructions
3. Do the work
4. Tap "Mark Complete"
5. Add a note if needed (e.g., "Couldn't finish, need supplies")

**Nap-time tasks:** The system suggests admin tasks you can knock out during nap time. Use this window for meal count submissions, photo uploads, and classroom prep.

### Incident Reporting Quick Reference

File an incident report within 30 minutes of any incident. Do not wait until end of day.

**What to report:**
- Any injury (even minor bumps, scrapes, bites)
- Illness symptoms (fever, vomiting, rash)
- Behavioral incidents (hitting, biting between children)
- Medication errors
- Allergic reactions
- Property damage
- Any situation a parent should know about

**Severity levels:**

| Level | Examples | Parent Notification |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | Small scrape, bump, minor fall | End-of-day notification |
| Moderate | Bite mark, persistent crying, mild allergic reaction | Same-day notification |
| Serious | Head injury, broken skin, breathing difficulty, ER visit | Immediate notification + phone call |

**How to file:**
1. Go to Dashboard > Incidents > New Report
2. Select the child, date, time, and location
3. Choose severity level
4. Describe what happened (facts only, no opinions)
5. Describe first aid given
6. Tap Submit

The system automatically notifies the director. Parent notification happens based on severity level. Every edit to the report is tracked, so write it right the first time.

### Certification Tracking

**Go to:** Dashboard > My Profile > Certifications

Your certifications page shows:
- Current certifications and their expiry dates
- Training hours logged this year
- Alerts at 90, 60, and 30 days before expiration

**What the alerts mean:**
- **Green** -- Current. No action needed.
- **Yellow** -- Expiring within 90 days. Start planning renewal.
- **Orange** -- Expiring within 60 days. Schedule your renewal now.
- **Red** -- Expiring within 30 days or expired. Urgent. You may not be able to work if this lapses.

**Your responsibility:** Check this page once a month. If anything is yellow or below, talk to your director about renewal options.
