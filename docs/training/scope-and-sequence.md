# Platform Training: Scope and Sequence

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** April 2026
**Prepared for:** Christina (Owner/Director), Center Leadership, Staff, Families
**Platform:** christinas-childcare.vercel.app

---

## How to Read This Document

This is the week-by-week training rollout for the Christina's Child Care Center platform. It covers all four role pathways: Owner, Director, Employee, and Parent/Family.

Print this and post it in the staff room. Each person finds their role, follows their pathway, and checks off weeks as they go.

**Start date assumption:** Monday, April 13, 2026. Adjust all dates if your start date is different.

---

## Rollout Strategy: Who Gets Trained First (and Why)

| Phase | Who | Weeks | Why This Order |
|-------|-----|-------|----------------|
| Phase 0 | Christina (Owner) | Weeks 0-7 | You need to know everything before you train anyone else. You are the expert, the troubleshooter, the answer person. |
| Phase 1 | Directors / Lead Teachers | Weeks 1-6 | Directors co-facilitate staff training. They need to be 2 weeks ahead of employees at all times. |
| Phase 2 | Employees (Cohorts of 3-4) | Weeks 3-6 | Small cohorts so the center stays staffed during training. Staff must be confident before parents ask questions. |
| Phase 3 | Parents / Families | Weeks 7-8 | Parents are the last group onboarded. Staff must be fluent so they can answer questions at drop-off and pick-up. |

**The logic is simple:** information flows downhill. Christina learns first, teaches directors, directors support employees, employees support parents. Nobody trains someone on a tool they haven't used themselves.

## Pathway 4: Owner/Admin (Christina)
### 30 Modules over 8 Weeks

Christina trains first, alone or with a facilitator. This is the deepest pathway. Everything the center runs on, Christina should be able to operate, explain, and troubleshoot.

**Total time commitment:** ~24 hours across 8 weeks (about 3 hours/week)
**Session format:** Mix of self-paced (evenings, weekends OK) and facilitated sessions (scheduled with a coach or self-guided with the facilitator guide)

---

#### Week 0: Foundation + Daily Rhythms
**Dates:** April 13-17
**Modules:** M01 Welcome, M02 Navigation, M03 Profile, M04 Kiosk, M05 Attendance, M06 Meal Counts
**Format:** Intensive orientation, 3 hours facilitated + 1 hour self-paced
**Time:** ~4 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Platform account created, login credentials in hand
**Materials:** Laptop or tablet, printed quick-start card, kiosk device for practice
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Logged in successfully to admin portal
- [ ] Navigated to 5 different pages in under 2 minutes
- [ ] Profile 100% complete
- [ ] Demonstrated kiosk check-in with demo PIN
- [ ] Viewed real-time attendance dashboard
- [ ] Submitted practice meal counts for all 3 meals

---

#### Week 1: Operations + Communication
**Dates:** April 20-24
**Modules:** M07 Photos, M08 Tasks, M09 Daily Reports, M10 Messaging, M11 Newsletters
**Format:** Self-paced (1.5 hrs) + scenario practice (1 hr facilitated)
**Time:** ~2.5 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Week 0 complete
**Materials:** Phone for photo upload practice, sample newsletter template
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Uploaded 3 tagged photos
- [ ] Created and assigned a task to yourself
- [ ] Viewed a child's daily report
- [ ] Sent a practice message
- [ ] Created a newsletter draft with 3+ sections

---

#### Week 2: Compliance (Full Unit)
**Dates:** April 27 - May 1
**Modules:** M14 CACFP Compliance, M15 Ratios, M16 Incidents, M17 Certifications
**Format:** Facilitated PD session (90 min) + self-paced follow-up (1.5 hr)
**Time:** ~3 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Weeks 0-1 complete; understand meal count submission from M06
**Materials:** Current CACFP documentation, staff certification records, incident report samples
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Achieved 90%+ on CACFP compliance dashboard
- [ ] Read ratio dashboard and identified a simulated violation
- [ ] Filed a complete practice incident report with parent notification
- [ ] All personal certifications entered and verified in system
- [ ] Passed 5-question CACFP knowledge check (80%+ required)

---

#### Week 3: Staff Management
**Dates:** May 4-8
**Modules:** M18 Scheduling, M19 HR Documents, M20 Payroll, M21 Staff Onboarding
**Format:** Facilitated HR/Scheduling workshop (90 min) + self-paced (2 hr)
**Time:** ~3.5 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Weeks 0-2 complete; employee records loaded into system
**Materials:** Current staff schedule, payroll records, onboarding checklist, HR templates
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Built a compliant one-week schedule for both centers
- [ ] Created an HR document from template
- [ ] Processed a practice payroll run with correct calculations
- [ ] Built a new hire onboarding pathway with checklist

---

#### Week 4: Growth + Enrollment
**Dates:** May 11-15
**Modules:** M22 Enrollment Pipeline, M23 Tour Management, M24 Authorization Tracking, M25 Website/Marketing
**Format:** Facilitated enrollment strategy session (60 min) + self-paced (2 hr)
**Time:** ~3 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Weeks 0-3 complete
**Materials:** Current enrollment waitlist, tour schedule, authorization records
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Moved 3 mock prospects through the enrollment pipeline (inquiry to enrolled)
- [ ] Scheduled and completed a mock tour using the digital checklist
- [ ] Identified all authorizations expiring within 60 days
- [ ] Reviewed public website pages and enrollment form submissions

---

#### Week 5: Financial Intelligence
**Dates:** May 18-22
**Modules:** M26 Budget Planning, M27 Revenue Forecasting, M28 Cost Optimization
**Format:** Facilitated financial PD session (90 min) + self-paced (1.5 hr)
**Time:** ~3 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Weeks 0-4 complete; actual financial data loaded (or realistic estimates)
**Materials:** Current budget, P&L statement, enrollment numbers, payroll totals
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Set annual budget by category
- [ ] Reviewed budget vs. actual and identified variances over 10%
- [ ] Ran a "what-if" scenario (3 children leave next month)
- [ ] Identified 3 cost optimization opportunities from platform data

---

#### Week 6: Strategic Leadership
**Dates:** May 25-29
**Modules:** M29 Cross-Site Operations, M30 Strategic Planning
**Format:** Facilitated strategic planning retreat (90 min) + self-paced (1 hr)
**Time:** ~2.5 hours total
**Prerequisites:** Weeks 0-5 complete
**Materials:** Mission/vision/values draft, SWOT notes, meeting agenda
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Monitored both centers from cross-site dashboard
- [ ] Completed SWOT analysis in system
- [ ] Set 3 strategic priorities with timelines and accountability dates
- [ ] Created action items that feed the task board

---

#### Week 7: Capstone
**Dates:** June 1-5
**Modules:** Full scenario simulation across all 30 modules
**Format:** Facilitated capstone exercise (2 hr)
**Time:** ~2 hours
**Prerequisites:** All previous weeks complete
**Materials:** Scenario packet (provided in facilitator guide)
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Passed all knowledge checks (80%+ on every unit)
- [ ] Completed personal growth plan
- [ ] Demonstrated 5 competencies from owner rubric with a partner or facilitator
- [ ] Ran a full simulated day on the platform with zero paper fallback
- [ ] Can explain any feature to a staff member or parent

## Pathway 3: Director / Lead Teacher
### 24 Modules over 6 Weeks

Directors start one week after Christina. They learn the same foundation skills, plus operations, compliance, scheduling, and enrollment. They do NOT learn payroll, financial intelligence, or strategic leadership (those are owner-only).

**Total time commitment:** ~16 hours across 6 weeks (about 2.5 hours/week)
**Session format:** Hybrid. Self-paced modules for foundation skills; facilitated sessions for compliance, scheduling, and enrollment.
**Who facilitates:** Christina (after completing her own Week 0)

---

**Total time commitment:** ~7 hours across 4 weeks (about 1.75 hours/week)
**Session format:** Mix of self-paced (can do on break or at home) and facilitated (30-45 min group sessions during nap time or before/after shift)

## Pathway 1: Parent / Family
### 8 Modules over 2 Weeks

Parents are the last group trained. By this point, every staff member can answer parent questions confidently.

**Total time commitment:** ~1.5 hours across 2 weeks (about 45 min/week)
**Session format:** Fully self-paced. Paper welcome packet provided at drop-off. Optional 15-minute demo at pick-up for families who want hands-on help.
**Who supports:** Any trained staff member at the front desk

---

#### Week 7 (their Week 1): Welcome + Kiosk
**Dates:** June 1-5
**Modules:** M01 Welcome, M02 Navigation, M03 Profile, M04 Kiosk
**Format:** Self-paced with paper welcome packet (30 min) + facilitated kiosk demo at drop-off (5 min per family)
**Time:** ~45 minutes total
**Prerequisites:** Family account created by director, PIN assigned
**Materials:** Printed welcome packet with login info, PIN card, kiosk reference card
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Logged into parent portal
- [ ] Family profile 100% complete (children, allergies, emergency contacts)
- [ ] Demonstrated kiosk check-in with their PIN

---

#### Week 8 (their Week 2): Portal + Communication
**Dates:** June 8-12
**Modules:** M10 Messaging, M12 Parent Portal Mastery, M13 Notifications, M09 Daily Reports (view only)
**Format:** Self-paced (30 min) + optional group demo at pick-up (15 min)
**Time:** ~45 minutes total
**Prerequisites:** Week 1 of pathway complete
**Materials:** Portal reference card, daily report viewing guide
**Completion gate:**
- [ ] Sent a message to child's teacher
- [ ] Viewed child's daily report (meals, naps, activities)
- [ ] Configured notification preferences
- [ ] Knows where to find photos, calendar, and family info

## Notes for the Facilitator

1. **Don't rush the kiosk.** Every single person who walks into the building needs to be comfortable with the kiosk. Budget extra time here. Practice with real children present.

2. **Meal counts are money.** M06 is the single highest-ROI module. A center with 40 kids that misses one meal count per week loses $6,000-16,000/year. Drill this until it's automatic.

3. **Cohort training during nap time works.** Schedule 30-45 minute facilitated sessions during the nap window (12:30-2:00). Keep one staff member on the floor; rotate the rest through training.

4. **Parents will have questions at drop-off.** The first Monday after parent onboarding (June 15), station someone at the kiosk to help. By the second week, most families will be independent.

5. **Print the completion gates.** Give each person a physical checklist for their pathway. Checking boxes is satisfying and makes progress visible.

6. **Celebrate the paper retirement.** When the center stops using paper sign-in sheets (target: June 15), mark the moment. It is a real operational change worth recognizing.
