# Christina's Child Care Center: Training Package

This is the complete training package for rolling out the Christina's Child Care Center platform across both sites (Brooklyn Park and Crystal). It covers every portal, every friction tool, and every daily workflow your team will use. The package is built for four roles, scaffolded from simplest to most advanced, so each person learns exactly what they need and nothing they don't.

---

## Who This Is For

| Role | What They Need to Know |
|------|----------------------|
| **Parent/Family** | Check in at the kiosk, view daily reports, message staff, manage their child's profile |
| **Employee/Staff** | Run daily operations: attendance, meal counts, photos, tasks, incidents, certifications |
| **Director/Lead Teacher** | Everything staff do, plus scheduling, compliance, enrollment pipeline, newsletters, HR |
| **Owner/Admin** | The full system: financials, forecasting, cost optimization, cross-site operations, strategic planning |

## How to Use This Package

**If you're the person delivering training** (owner, director, or outside facilitator), follow this sequence:

1. **Read `curriculum-guide.md` first.** This is the anchor document. It gives you the full picture of all 30 modules, what each one teaches, and why it matters financially.

2. **Pick your audience.** Open `scope-and-sequence.md` and find the pathway for the role you're training. Each pathway has a week-by-week schedule with session lengths.

3. **Prep your sessions.** For facilitated modules (marked in the scope and sequence), open `facilitator-guide.md` and pull the session plan. For self-paced modules, hand out the matching paper packet.

4. **Print the paper packet.** Go to `packets/` and print the role-specific packet. These are designed for 8.5x11 paper. Hand them out on day one.

5. **Run the training.** Follow the weekly schedule. Use the unit detail files in `modules/` for step-by-step platform walkthroughs and discussion questions.

6. **Assess along the way.** Pull knowledge checks and scenarios from `assessment-bank.md`. Use `competency-rubrics.md` for hands-on skill verification.

7. **Track completion gates.** Each pathway ends with specific competency demonstrations (listed in `scope-and-sequence.md`). Nobody is "done" until they pass the gate.

## A Note on This Package

These documents are written in Markdown. You can read them as-is, convert them to PDF for printing, or paste them into any document editor for further customization. The paper packets in `packets/` are specifically formatted for print distribution.

Every module maps directly to pages and tools in the live platform. When the curriculum says "navigate to the attendance dashboard," that page exists and works. This is training for a real system, not a theoretical one.
