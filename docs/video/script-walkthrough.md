**SCREEN:** Scroll to `#replaces`. Slow scroll through the 14-row replacement table.
**VOICE:** This table is the most important section on the page. Every row is a manual process you do today and the platform feature that replaces it. Paper sign-in sheets become an iPad kiosk. ## Scene 8: Families and Staff (35 seconds)

**SCREEN:** Scroll to `#families`, pause on the 4 impact cards. Then scroll to `#staff`, pause on those 4 cards.
**VOICE:** For families: daily photos, self-service access, digital progress reports, real-time attendance visibility.
