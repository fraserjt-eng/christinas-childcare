# Christina's Child Care Center
## Integrated Technology & Operations Platform

### Executive Summary

Christina's Child Care Center now has a complete, custom-built technology platform that consolidates 5+ fragmented tools into one unified system. This platform includes 37+ integrated pages across Admin, Employee, and Parent portals, an AI-powered lesson builder, comprehensive training resources, and multi-location support ready for the Brooklyn Park expansion. Built by family who understands the business, this solution replaces scattered subscriptions with a single relationship—saving an estimated 12-16 hours per week and $550-1,230 per month in tool consolidation.
