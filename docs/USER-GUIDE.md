# Christina's Child Care Center — User Guide

This is every feature on the site, explained like you're showing it to someone for the first time.

---

## THE WEBSITE (what parents and visitors see)

### Home Page (/)
The front door. Shows what the center is about, why parents should trust you, what programs you offer, staff bios, and a news feed. There's a big "Schedule a Tour" button.

### About (/about)
Who Christina is, the center's story, philosophy. The "why us" page.

### Programs (/programs)
What you actually do with kids at each age: infants, toddlers, preschool, school age. Curriculum details, daily schedules, what a typical day looks like.

### Photo Gallery (/gallery)
Public photos from the center. Art projects, outdoor play, events. Makes parents feel good about enrolling.

### FAQ (/faq)
Common questions: hours, rates, what to bring, sick policies, food allergies, etc.

### Schedule a Tour (/schedule-tour)
Parents fill out their name, phone, email, pick a date and time. Christina gets notified. This is how new families start.

### Enroll (/enroll)
The enrollment form. Parents fill out child info, medical notes, emergency contacts. This is the application.

### Curriculum (/scope-sequence)
Detailed breakdown of what kids learn by age group and unit. For parents who want to see the educational plan.

### Signup Guide (/signup-guide)
Step-by-step instructions for parents: how to create an account, what to expect, what documents you need.

### Blog (/blog)
Articles about childcare, parenting tips, center updates. Good for SEO and showing expertise.

### Training Hub (/training, /training/guides, /training/scripts, /training/docs)
Public training resources. Could be used for prospective staff or existing staff who need reference materials.

## LOGGING IN

### Parent Login (/login)
Parents type their email and password. Demo account: `parent@demo.com` / `parent123`. Once logged in, they see their family dashboard.

### Employee Login (/employee-login)
Staff enter their PIN. It's a number pad, like clocking in at a register. Demo PINs: `1234` (Ophelia) or `2345` (Stephen). Fast, no typing an email.

### Admin Login (/admin-login)

## PARENT DASHBOARD (what families see after logging in)

### My Dashboard (/dashboard)
The parent home screen. Shows your kids, recent activity, quick links to photos, calendar, messages. Think of it like the main screen of a banking app but for your daycare.

### My Children (/dashboard/children)
Your enrolled kids. Each child shows their name, classroom, allergies, medical notes, emergency contacts. You can edit this info.

### Progress Reports (/dashboard/progress)
How your kid is doing. Developmental milestones, teacher observations, skill tracking. The report card for daycare.

### Photo Gallery (/dashboard/photos)
Daily photos from your child's classroom. Teachers upload them, Christina approves them, you see them here. Tap the heart to react. Filter by classroom.

### Newsletter (/dashboard/news)
Weekly newsletters from the center. Shows what happened this week, upcoming events, menu highlights, classroom spotlights. Searchable archive of all past newsletters.

### Documents (/dashboard/documents)
Important paperwork: policies, handbooks, permission slips, tax documents.

### Calendar (/dashboard/calendar)
Center events, closures, field trips, picture day. Everything with a date.

### Messages (/dashboard/messages)
Messages from the center to you. Announcements, individual notes from teachers, daily updates. You can see what you've read and what's new.

### Notification Preferences (/dashboard/notifications)
Choose how you hear from us:
- **Email**: toggle on/off, enter your email address
- **Text (SMS)**: toggle on/off, enter your phone number
- **Phone call**: toggle on/off, enter your phone number
- **What you want to hear about**: newsletters, announcements, incidents, closures, billing, photos (pick and choose)
- **How often**: immediately, daily digest, or weekly summary
- **Quiet hours**: don't bug me between 9 PM and 7 AM

## EMPLOYEE PORTAL (what staff see after PIN login)

### My Home (/employee)
The staff home screen. Six big tiles:
- **Clock In/Out**: tap to start or end your shift. Shows how long you've been working.
- **Meal Count**: enter how many kids ate at your table (the big one, explained more below)
- **Photos**: upload pictures from your classroom today
- **Chat**: messages with other staff
- **My Schedule**: your shifts this week
- **Training**: required training modules with completion status

Below the tiles: today's task checklist, weekly hours summary, quick links to time off, pay stubs, and profile.

### Meal Count (/employee/meal-count)
**This is the friction killer.** Instead of Christina typing 48 numbers into a spreadsheet every day:
1. Pick your classroom
2. See a big number (pre-filled from yesterday or 85% of capacity)
3. Tap + or - to adjust
4. Hit Submit
5. Done in 15 seconds

Shows which classrooms already reported (green checkmark) so Christina can see who's done without asking.

### Upload Photos (/employee/photos)
Take or upload photos from your classroom. Tag the activity type (art, outdoor play, circle time, etc.), pick your classroom, add a caption. Submit up to 5 at once. These go to Christina for approval before parents see them.

### Nap Time Tasks (/employee/nap-tasks)
Between 12:30 and 2:30, this shows the tasks you can do while kids sleep. Sorted by quickest first. Tap "Start" when you begin, "Done" when you finish. Progress bar shows how much of the 90-minute window you've used.

### My Tasks (/employee/tasks)
Your full task list. Everything assigned to you: daily routines, one-time items, recurring tasks. Check them off as you go.

### Knowledge Base (/employee/knowledge)
The center's wiki. How to do things: morning opening procedure, fire drill steps, how to mix formula, where the first aid kit is. If you know something that isn't written down, tap "Share What You Know" to submit it.

### My Development (/employee/development)
Your professional growth page:
- **My Certifications**: CPR, First Aid, Food Handler, State License. Shows if they're current (green), expiring soon (yellow), or expired (red) with exact dates.
- **Training Hours**: progress bar showing your hours this year vs the state requirement (usually 16 hours)
- **My Goals**: professional development goals set during reviews

### Request Supplies (/employee/supplies)
Need more paper towels? Art supplies running low? Fill out the form: what you need, which classroom, how urgent (need today / this week / routine). Christina gets the request and can approve it.

### My Schedule (/employee/schedule)
Your shifts this week. When you start, when you end, which classroom you're in.

### Pay Stubs (/employee/pay-stubs)
Your pay history. Hours worked, hourly rate, gross pay, deductions.

### Time Off (/employee/time-off)
Request a day off. Pick the date, the reason, submit. Christina approves or finds coverage.

### My Profile (/employee/profile)
Your contact info, emergency contact, job title. Keep it updated.

### Training (/employee/training)
Required training modules. Watch the video, read the material, mark as complete. Tracks what you've finished and what's still due.

### Staff Chat (/admin/messaging)
Group chat with other staff. Channels, direct messages, pinned announcements.

## ADMIN PORTAL (what Christina sees)

### TODAY GROUP

#### Dashboard (/admin)
Christina's command center. Shows:
- **Greeting**: "Good morning, Christina" with the current time zone (opening/core/closing)
- **Alerts**: red/yellow/blue cards for anything that needs attention right now (missed meal counts, expired certs, open incidents, unread messages)
- **Today's Snapshot**: kids present, staff on duty, ratio status, tasks done
- **Quick Actions**: the 4 most relevant buttons for this time of day (changes based on morning/afternoon/evening)

#### News & Updates (/admin/news)
Write and publish news updates that parents see on their dashboard. Announcements, reminders, seasonal updates.

#### Attendance (/admin/attendance)
Who's here today. List of all enrolled kids: checked in (green), not yet (gray), departed (blue). Tap to check someone in or out.

#### Ratio Monitor (/admin/ratios)
Are you legal? Shows staff-to-child ratio per classroom against state requirements. Green means good, red means you need another teacher in that room NOW.

#### Food Counts (/admin/food-counts)
The CACFP meal tracking hub. Six tabs:

- **Daily Counts**: the grid where meal counts go. BUT NOW with three magic buttons at the top:
  - **Copy Yesterday**: one click fills the whole grid from yesterday's numbers. Adjust the 2-3 that changed.
  - **Fill from Enrollment**: fills every classroom at 85% capacity. Close enough for most days.
  - **Everyone Here**: fills to max capacity. Adjust rooms that are short.

- **Compliance**: shows your on-time submission rate (%), revenue impact of missed counts, the full CACFP compliance checklist (16 items across 5 categories), audit readiness score (0-100%), and reimbursement tracker (expected vs actual money received)

- **CACFP Reports**: monthly totals by meal type, estimated reimbursement, export button

- **Projections**: forecasts future meal counts based on historical data

- **Cost Tracking**: food expenses by category

- **Settings**: manage classrooms (add, rename, set capacity)

### PEOPLE GROUP

#### Staff Directory (/admin/staff)
All your employees. Name, role, contact info, hire date, employment status.

#### Knowledge Base (/admin/staff/knowledge-base)
The center's brain. Write down how everything works:
- **Categories**: Daily Procedures, Emergency Protocols, Classroom Routines, Parent Communication Templates, Compliance Checklists, Vendor Contacts, Equipment Instructions
- **Rich text editor**: bold, italic, headings, lists, links, images (same toolbar as the newsletter builder)
- **Version history**: see every edit with timestamps, roll back if someone messes it up
- **Onboarding tag**: mark entries as "required reading for new staff"
- Staff can submit their own knowledge through the employee portal (you review and publish)

#### Staff Development (/admin/staff/development)
Three tabs:
- **Certifications**: grid showing every employee's CPR, First Aid, Food Handler, State License status. Green/yellow/red by expiry date. Click to update dates. Alerts at 90, 60, 30 days before expiration.
- **Training Log**: who took what training, when, how many hours, from which provider. Progress bars showing hours completed vs state requirement.
- **Development Plans**: goals for each employee. Set during reviews, track progress, mark complete.

#### Scheduling (/admin/scheduling)
Build the weekly schedule. Who works when, in which classroom.

#### Schedule Optimizer (/admin/schedule-optimizer)
Smart scheduling:
- **Weekly grid**: see all staff shifts at a glance, color-coded by center
- **Ratio compliance**: for each day, see if you have enough staff in every room. Red X = problem.
- **Labor cost**: weekly cost breakdown per employee, overtime alerts before they happen, planned vs actual hours
- **Coverage requests**: when staff request time off, see who can cover, approve with one click

#### Schedule Requests (/admin/schedule-requests)
Review staff schedule change requests. Approve or deny.

#### Salaried Staff (/admin/salaried-scheduling)
Separate scheduling view for salaried employees (different rules than hourly).

#### HR & Onboarding (/admin/hr)
Human resources hub.

#### Onboarding (/admin/hr/onboarding)
New hire checklist system:
- **Templates**: build onboarding checklists with 4 phases (Pre-Start, Day 1, Week 1, Month 1). Each task has a title, who's responsible, how many days after start it's due, and how to verify (self-check or supervisor sign-off).
- **Active Onboarding**: see all new hires in progress. Progress bars show if they're ahead, on track, behind, or blocked. Click to see their full checklist.

#### Compliance (/admin/compliance)
Tracking for background checks, certifications, state licensing requirements.

#### Payroll (/admin/payroll)
Pay management. Hours, rates, pay periods.

#### Inquiries (/admin/inquiries)
New family inquiries that came in. First step in the enrollment funnel.

#### Pipeline (/admin/pipeline)
Enrollment pipeline overview. Where every prospective family is in the process.

#### Authorizations (/admin/pipeline/authorizations)
State authorization tracking for enrolled children:
- **Grid**: every child's authorization type, start/end dates, days remaining, status
- **Color-coded**: Active (green), Expiring Soon (yellow, <30 days), Expired (red), Pending (blue)
- **Sorted by urgency**: most urgent expirations at the top
- **Renewal tracker**: which renewals are in progress, average processing time, revenue at risk from expired authorizations
- The dashboard alerts Christina automatically at 60, 30, and 14 days before any authorization expires

#### Enrollment Funnel (/admin/pipeline/enrollment)
The sales pipeline for new families:
- **Funnel chart**: visual showing how many families are at each stage (Inquiry > Tour Scheduled > Tour Completed > Application > Waitlist > Enrolled > Active) with conversion rates between stages
- **Pipeline board**: card view of every lead, organized by stage. Yellow border if no activity in 7+ days (stale lead). Click a card to see contact info, advance the stage, add notes.
- **Lead sources**: pie chart showing where families heard about you (website, referral, drive-by, social media). Shows which sources actually convert to enrolled families.
- **Revenue projection**: how much money is in the pipeline. Each stage has a probability weight. Shows projected monthly revenue, current enrollment vs capacity, gap to fill.

#### Tour Manager (/admin/pipeline/tours)
Make every tour the same great experience:
- **Tour Calendar**: upcoming tours, schedule new ones (parent name, contact, center, date, time slot)
- **Tour Checklist**: when giving a tour, follow the 8-step script (warm greeting, safety overview, classroom visits, Q&A). Check off each step, timer tracks how long the tour takes. "Complete Tour" button when done.
- **Follow-up Tracking**: which completed tours haven't gotten a follow-up message yet. One-click send. Tracks the conversion: tour > follow-up sent > application received.

### OPERATIONS GROUP

#### Task Board (/admin/tasks)
The master to-do system. Multiple views:
- **List view**: all tasks with priority, status, assigned to, due date, category
- **Kanban**: drag-and-drop board with To Do, In Progress, Done columns. Cards show task name, time estimate, who it's assigned to, category badge.
- **Timeline**: visual daily schedule from 6 AM to 6 PM. Tasks placed in 30-minute time blocks, color-coded by category. Current time indicator shows where you are in the day.
- **Insights**: auto-generated cards like "You spent 12 hours on compliance this week, 3x more than operations" or "8 tasks were delegatable but still done by you"
- **Delegation tracker**: pie chart of Christina's tasks vs delegated tasks. Delegation score (0-100). Lists of "only Christina can do" vs "could be delegated."
- **Nap time optimizer**: during 12:30-2:30, shows which tasks fit the 90-minute window, sorted by priority then shortest first. "What can I finish in 30 minutes?" filter.
- **Staff capability matrix**: grid showing which staff can handle which task categories (based on completion history). Identifies bottlenecks: "Only Christina and Maria can handle compliance tasks."

#### Task Assessment (/admin/tasks/assessment)
Observation and assessment tool for evaluating task completion quality.

#### Cross-Site Operations (/admin/operations)
See both centers at once without driving between them:
- **Live Status**: side-by-side cards for Crystal Center and Brooklyn Park Center. Each shows: kids present, staff on duty, staff-child ratio (green/red), open incidents, pending messages, supply requests. Auto-refreshes.
- **Weekly Trends**: line charts showing attendance and ratio patterns per center over the past 5 days. Spot patterns like "Brooklyn Park runs low-staffed on Fridays."

#### Curriculum (/admin/curriculum)
Manage the educational curriculum. Units, lessons, learning objectives by age group.

#### Lesson Builder (/admin/lessons)
Create and edit lesson plans. Templates, materials lists, learning outcomes.

#### Daily Reports (/admin/reports)
End-of-day reports from classrooms. What happened, what kids did, any concerns.

#### Inventory (/admin/inventory)
The original inventory page (food and supplies tracking).

#### Supply Management (/admin/supplies)
Enhanced supply tracking:
- **Inventory List**: all items grouped by category (classroom, cleaning, food/kitchen, office, first aid, outdoor). Each shows quantity with color (green = good, yellow = low, red = critical), vendor, cost. Quick +1/-1 buttons to adjust counts.
- **Staff Requests**: pending supply requests from teachers. See who needs what, how urgent. Fulfill or deny with one click.
- **Reorder List**: auto-generated list of everything below its minimum threshold. Check what you want to order, see the estimated cost, copy the order to clipboard for emailing your vendor.
- **Spend Chart**: monthly supply spending by category over 6 months. See if costs are going up or down.

#### Menu Planning (/admin/menu-planning)
Plan weekly menus. What's for breakfast, lunch, snacks each day.

#### Incidents (/admin/incidents)
The original incident reporting page.

#### Incident Log (/admin/incidents/log)
Enhanced incident system:
- **Report Incident**: structured form that won't let you skip the important stuff. Date, time, child, classroom, type (injury/illness/behavioral/property), severity, what happened, what you did about it, witnesses, staff present. Required: did you tell the parent? Photo upload for documentation.
- **Timeline**: chronological list of all incidents. Filter by type, severity, or whether parents were notified. Red warning flag if a parent wasn't told within 24 hours.
- **Analytics**: charts showing incident patterns. Which classrooms have the most? What time of day? Are incidents trending up or down? What percentage have complete documentation?

#### Incident Training (/admin/incidents/training)
Training modules specifically about incident handling and documentation.

### COMMUNICATIONS GROUP

#### Staff Chat (/admin/messaging)
Internal messaging. Channels (like Slack), direct messages, pinned important messages, reactions, read receipts.

#### Newsletters (/admin/communications)
The Mailchimp-style newsletter builder:
- **Rich text editor**: bold, italic, underline, headings, lists, links, images, text alignment, undo/redo. Full toolbar.
- **Drag-and-drop sections**: grab a section, drag it up or down to reorder. Section types: Photo Highlights, Upcoming Events, Menu Highlights, Classroom Spotlight, Milestones, Announcements, Custom.

- **PDF export**: one click downloads a formatted PDF of the newsletter.
- **Schedule or send now**: pick a date/time for future delivery, or send immediately.
- **Templates**: save and reuse newsletter layouts.
- **Analytics**: after sending, see which families opened it, which sections they clicked, device breakdown (mobile vs desktop), engagement over time.

#### Photo Review (/admin/communications/photos)
Review photos uploaded by staff before parents see them:
- Grid of today's photos. Each shows the image, classroom, activity type, who uploaded it, when.
- **Approve** or **Reject** each photo.
- **Bulk actions**: select multiple photos, approve or reject all at once.
- **Filters**: show all, pending only, approved, rejected.
- **Stats bar**: total uploaded today, pending review, approved, rejected.

#### Notifications (/admin/notifications)
Admin notification center. System alerts, broadcast messages.

#### Meetings (/admin/meetings)
The original meetings page.

#### Meeting Efficiency (/admin/meetings/efficiency)
Run better meetings:
- **Plan Meeting**: create a meeting with title, date, time, attendees. Build the agenda: each item has a topic, how many minutes, who's presenting, and purpose (inform / discuss / decide). Total time calculator warns if your agenda is too long for the time slot.
- **Active Meeting**: when a meeting is in progress, this is the live cockpit:
  - Overall timer counting up
  - Per-agenda-item countdown (green when under time, yellow at 75%, red when over)
  - "Next Item" button to advance through the agenda
  - Real-time notes (rich text editor)
  - Decision capture: "We decided X" with who owns it
  - Action item capture: "Do X" with owner and due date
  - "End Meeting" calculates effectiveness score
- **Meeting History**: past meetings with actual vs planned time, notes, decisions, action items, effectiveness score
- **Action Items**: every action item from every meeting in one place. Filter by overdue, pending, completed. Shows which meeting it came from, who owns it, when it's due. One-click complete button.

## HOW IT ALL CONNECTS

The tools aren't standalone. They feed into each other:

- Teachers enter **meal counts** from their phone > Christina sees compliance on her dashboard > missed counts trigger **CACFP alerts** > the reimbursement tracker shows lost money
- Teachers upload **photos** > Christina approves them > they appear in the **parent photo feed** > they auto-populate into the **weekly newsletter** > analytics track which parents engaged
- **Certifications** expire > alert appears on Christina's **dashboard** > she checks **staff development** > assigns training through the **knowledge base**
- New family fills out **tour form** > appears in **tour scheduler** > checklist guides the tour > follow-up tracks conversion > enrolled family enters the **enrollment funnel** > **authorization tracking** monitors their state paperwork > **revenue forecasting** projects the financial impact
- **Meeting** produces action items > they appear on the **task board** > **delegation engine** suggests who should do them > **nap time optimizer** schedules the quick ones > **time insights** show if Christina is still doing everything herself
