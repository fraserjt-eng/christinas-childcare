# Illustration Brief — Platform Reel v1

Five cartoon frames. Consistent art direction. Same character across all frames. Storybook register, not corporate.

## Art direction

- Hand-drawn cartoon. Soft rounded lines, flat shading with a single soft shadow pass.
- Warm palette. Cream background (`#f5f0e8`) with red (`#C62828`) and yellow (`#FFD54F`) accents. Never neon, never cold blues.
- Character: medium-brown woman in her mid-forties, warm face, simple cardigan or blouse, hair up. Keep her visually identical across every frame.
- Children in the frames are cartoon kids, no specific features that identify a real child.
- No corporate logos, no Apple or Google device brands in the illustrations.

## Aspect ratio

All frames at `1080 x 1920` (9:16). Compose so the key subject sits in the middle third so the composition still reads when cropped to 1:1 or 16:9.

## Five frames

### Frame 01 — Chaos wide
**Prompt skeleton:**
"Hand-drawn cartoon, warm cream background, friendly medium-brown woman in her mid-forties, hair up, simple cardigan, sitting at a cluttered wooden desk. Phones ringing, papers flying mid-air, a spilled coffee cup, a small cartoon child tugging at her sleeve. Expression: overwhelmed but not defeated. Soft shadows, storybook feel. Ratio 9:16. Leave top third empty for a headline."

### Frame 02 — Chaos tight
**Prompt skeleton:**
"Same woman, same style as frame 01, tighter crop on her face and hands. Thought bubble above her head with rough pencil texture. Rough papers still flying. Ratio 9:16. Leave top quarter empty for a thought-bubble caption."

### Frame 03 — Calm parallel
**Prompt skeleton:**
"Same woman, same style. Clean quiet office inside a child care center. Open laptop showing a simple scheduling grid. A child beside her smiling at a book. A parent at the door waving. Warm afternoon light. Ratio 9:16. Leave top third empty for a short headline."

### Frame 04 — Brand card
**Prompt skeleton:**
"A warm cream rectangular card, rounded corners, soft drop shadow, centered on a blurred cream background. Inside the card: a square headshot area on the left (leave blank, real headshot will be composited in), name and title on the right in clean sans-serif type, a short horizontal red rule beneath. Ratio 9:16. Card should occupy the middle third."

### Frame 05 — Platform outcomes
**Prompt skeleton:**
"Same woman, same style. Soft top-down illustration of a simple child care classroom floor plan underneath. Three short labeled tags floating above the floor plan. Tags are warm cream rectangles with a tiny red icon on the left. Ratio 9:16. Leave bottom fifth empty for a URL footer."

## What not to draw

- No real children.
- No specific faces other than Christina's (her real headshot is composited in separately on frame 04).
- No text baked into the illustration. All text is added in Remotion.
- No exact Playground-style illustrations. Different palette, different character, different feel.
- No chaotic color combinations. Warmth everywhere.
