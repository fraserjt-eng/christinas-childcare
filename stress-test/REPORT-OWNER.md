## What Needs Work

### The platform can't tell you room ratios in real time
When a child checks in at the kiosk, the system records that they arrived. But it doesn't track which classroom they went to. So if a licensor walks in and says "show me your infant room ratio right now," the platform can't answer that directly. **This is the most important fix.**

### Incidents don't connect to the same child over time
The biting report notes this is the "second time this month" because a human typed that. But the system itself doesn't know that. It can't tell you: "Zion has had 3 behavioral incidents in the last 60 days." If you need to have a parent conference, you're still relying on memory, not data.

### No end-of-day summary that puts the whole day together
Your attendance data is in one place. Meals in another. Incidents in another. Staffing in another. There's no single "here's what happened today" view that pulls it all together for you before you go home.

### Emergency contacts aren't on file for any staff
The fields exist in the system, but nobody's emergency contact information has been entered. If a licensor checks, this is a finding.

### The platform doesn't prepare you for tomorrow
It can tell you what happened today. It cannot tell you: "Tomorrow you have 3 staff scheduled, but based on this week's attendance pattern, you'll probably have 48 kids. You need a floater."
