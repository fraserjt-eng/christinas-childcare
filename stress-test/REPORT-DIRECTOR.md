## Things You Should Know About This Platform

These are capabilities you might not have discovered yet:

1. **The intelligence dashboard** (`/admin/intelligence`) scans your training data and staffing data automatically. It can tell you which staff members are falling behind on modules and which certifications are clustering around the same expiration date.

2. **Every incident has a follow-up flag.** When you mark an incident as "follow-up required," it stays visible until you close it. Use this. Don't let incidents close without documented resolution.

3. **The enrollment pipeline** tracks families from first inquiry to first day of attendance. If a family goes 7+ days without moving to the next stage, that's a lead going cold. Check it weekly.

4. **Meal counts track on-time submission.** CACFP auditors care about whether counts were recorded at the time of service or after the fact. Your platform timestamps every submission. Keep using the real-time entry, not end-of-day backfill.

5. **The financial dashboard** shows your revenue breakdown by tuition vs. CACFP. Right now CACFP is 7.4% of your revenue. Industry average is 8-12%. You may be under-claiming. Worth reviewing with Christina.
