Christina's Child Care Center

How sign-in flows through everything

Two simple actions run the whole center: staff clocking in and out, and children checking in and out. Here is exactly where each one goes, so nothing has to be entered twice.

For staff

Teachers, leads, and admin

You enter your PIN and clock in

Your start time is recorded against your name, on the device in your room. You do not log it anywhere else.

Your time flows out automatically

The same clock-in feeds every place your hours matter:

Your hours and pay stub Who is on the floor Staff-to-child ratios Labor on the budget The owner dashboard Daily reports

You clock out

Your hours close for the day and your pay is calculated from the real times, not an estimate.

Made a mistake, or forgot to clock out? A manager can correct your clock-in or clock-out time. Your hours and pay update right away.

For families

Parents and guardians

Your child checks in at the kiosk

You enter your family PIN. The arrival time is recorded for your child. That is the only step.

It flows where it is needed

Your child's check-in updates the center in real time:

Marked present in the room Counted in the ratio check The owner dashboard Today's reports Their daily report

Your child checks out

Pickup time is recorded and your child is marked as departed for the day. You only ever see your own child.

If a check-in or check-out is missed or wrong, staff can correct the time so your child's record and the room count stay accurate.
